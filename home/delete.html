Delete
If you confirm that you need to delete your account, please send an email to support@ringo.cool. In the email, your uid and nickname need to be included.
After you submit the account deletion request, the deletion process will start after 30 days. During this period, your account will be deactivated and you will not be able to access it. You can cancel the deletion request at any time within these 30 days. If it is not cancelled within 30 days, your account will be permanently deleted on the 30th day after the request is submitted.
